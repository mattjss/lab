Vector A 16×16 grid of bars that rotate in unison to face your cursor like compass needles. Click fires a rotational shockwave that ripples across the field.

about

Curious what would happen if every element on screen reacted to your cursor at once. Move around and they all follow — some snap fast, others drift depending on how close you are. Click anywhere to throw them off before they recover.

about

Started as an interaction experiment and turned into a game. Move your cursor toward the birds to scare them and guide the flock into the glowing ring to score. Lose too many off the edge and it's game over — the last visitor's score shows up when you load it.

about

Move your cursor across the surface and it pushes the fabric out of shape. The threads under the most stress glow brighter as tension builds. Click to shock the whole thing at once and watch it ripple back into place.

about

Move around and the nearest dots stretch toward you, forming a web of legs around your cursor. Left click and a ripple travels outward through the whole grid, displacing everything in its path. Right click to swap colors.
