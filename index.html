Interactive experiments · move + click each to test

Vector A grid of bars that all track your cursor. Click to fire a shockwave through the field.
DOM · CSS · angular spring →

Wanted to see what 256 simple elements could do if they all responded to the same input. Each bar rotates on a spring so they have physical weight — nearby ones snap fast, far ones drift. Click anywhere to scramble the whole field before they recover.
Flock Herd a flock of birds into a target ring using your cursor as a predator.
canvas · boids · game →

This one turned into a little game. 150 boids running flocking rules, all scared of your cursor — chase them into the glowing ring to score. If they escape the canvas they're gone for good, and the last visitor's score shows up when you load it.
Cloth A spring mesh you can poke and deform. Threads under tension glow, click sends a wave through it.
canvas · spring physics →

A 22×22 grid of particles connected by springs, each anchored to a resting position. Push nodes out of place and the connections under stress light up as tension builds. Click to send a shockwave through the whole grid at once.
Grid Tick marks that rotate to face your cursor. Click fires a rotational wave across the field.
canvas · angular spring →

Like Vector but built in canvas — 26×26 marks each running as a damped pendulum tracking the cursor. Nearby ones react fast, distant ones lag, which creates a rolling wave effect just from moving around. Click to fire a staggered impulse outward from the impact point.
Spider Mesh A particle mesh that clings to your cursor like a web. Left click fires a ripple, right click cycles the color.
canvas · spider mesh · ripple →

Move your cursor and nearby nodes stretch toward it, forming a spider-leg pattern around you. Left click sends a ripple wave through the whole mesh — every node displaces as it passes and shimmers in the wake. Right click to cycle through colors.
